Countries and Capitals

United States

Capital: Washington, D.C.

The United States is known for its diverse geography, technology, and cultural influence. Washington, D.C. serves as the nation's capital and is home to numerous iconic landmarks like the White House and the U.S. Capitol.

France

Capital: Paris

France is famous for its rich history, culture, and world-renowned cuisine. Paris, the capital, is known as the "City of Light" and is home to landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

Japan

Capital: Tokyo

Japan is a country known for its technological advancements, rich culture, and beautiful landscapes. Tokyo, the capital, is a bustling metropolis famous for its skyscrapers, shopping districts, and ancient temples.

Germany

Capital: Berlin

Germany is known for its rich history, engineering, and strong economy. Berlin, the capital, is famous for its cultural landmarks like the Brandenburg Gate, the Berlin Wall, and the Museum Island.

Armenia

Capital: Yerevan

Armenia is a country rich in history, ancient monuments, and beautiful landscapes. Yerevan, the capital, is known for its vibrant culture, historical sites, and modern architecture.

Russia

Capital: Moscow

Russia is the largest country in the world, known for its vast landscapes, historical cities, and cultural heritage. Moscow, the capital, is home to landmarks like the Kremlin and Red Square.

United Kingdom

Capital: London

The United Kingdom is known for its monarchy, historical sites, and global influence. London, the capital, features iconic landmarks such as the Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and the Tower of London.
